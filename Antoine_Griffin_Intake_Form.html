Quality Tips

  • Natural window light is perfect. Avoid harsh overhead fluorescent.
  • Plain walls or simple backgrounds work best — AI generates the environments.
  • No filters. Raw, unedited photos only.
  • Phone camera is fine — iPhone rear camera in good light works great.
  • Multiple sessions are fine, but ONLY if the hairstyle is identical each time.
